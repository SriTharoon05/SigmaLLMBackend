- Difficulty tracking fee payments by center, class level, or student category
- Lack of visual indicators for different fee types making performance assessment difficult
- Delayed insights on student dues impacting timely follow-ups and financial forecasting\n\nSolution: - Implemented a real-time analytics dashboard using Microsoft Power BI
- Created live visualization of fee payments versus dues updated automatically
- Added filters by school center, class, and academic year for focused data review
- Broke down fee data by types such as tuition, admission, and examination fees
- Visualized payment frequency types (monthly, one-time, termly, yearly) with bar charts
- Enabled student-level tracking with detailed payment records
- Included year-on-year comparison tools for fee performance
- Designed solution for multi-campus scalability including Kenyan and Liberian schools\n\nSolution Benefits: - Centralized fee reporting across all locations reducing manual consolidation
- Enabled data-driven communication with parents regarding fee status
- Improved oversight of fee collection with real-time tracking
- Facilitated early detection of payment irregularities like duplicates or unrecorded dues
