Case Study Name: PAP&PAT-CN-Improving Delivery Times and Productivity using Microsoft Power Apps & Power Automate-Case Study\n\nCase Study Details:\n\nIndustry: Construction\n\nChallenges: - Heavy reliance on Excel spreadsheets and template-based processes
- Tedious and repetitive manual Extra Purchase Order’s (EPO) creation leading to project delays
- Manual management of different templates for different EPO processes
- High lead time for approval process causing delays
- Inability to track and monitor purchase orders once submitted
- Manual update required of the HSP legacy system once EPO was approved
- Administrative delays due to lack of available staff and resources\n\nSolution: - Designed, developed, and implemented an Extra Purchase Order (EPO) Automation solution using Microsoft Power Apps and Power Automate (RPA)
- Automated data retrieval from the legacy AS/400-based HSP system
- Implemented robotic keystrokes for reinputting details in the HSP legacy system
- Created customizable EPO request forms and approval processes
- Integrated with HSP legacy application for accounting continuity
- Developed automated approval process notifications\n\nSolution Benefits: - Reduced time for manual entry and processing of EPOs by up to 50%
- Enhanced visibility into Purchase Orders, improving tracking and approval times
- Provided real-time status updates on Purchase Orders from various vendors
- Improved human resource allocation, allowing administrative staff to prioritize additional tasks
- Reduced labor costs by avoiding overtime accrual\n\nKey Phrases: Extra Purchase Order (EPO), Microsoft Power Apps, Microsoft Power Automate, RPA, AS/400, HSP legacy system, automated approval workflows, real-time status updates, labor cost reduction, operational efficiency\n\nTechnology: Microsoft Power Apps, Microsoft Power Automate, Microsoft Power BI, Microsoft Power Virtual Agents\n\nClient Location: Texas
