Case Study Name: PBI-SH-Implementation of an organization-wide Power BI solution for the shipping Industry-Case Study\n\nCase Study Details:\n\nIndustry: Shipping\n\nChallenges: - Legacy systems in place leading to inefficiencies.
- Non-availability of analytics platforms for standard reporting.
- Lack of a consolidated reporting platform for automation.
- Absence of a centralized reporting portal for detailed analysis.
- Difficulty in generating real-time reports in finance and operations.
- Challenges in monitoring and managing daily operational reports.\n\nSolution: - Implemented an organization-wide Business Intelligence (BI) system using Power BI.
- Identified data sources for reporting.
- Automated ETL processes for data handling.
- Created a data warehouse for centralized data storage.
- Prepared data for Power BI reports and dashboards.
- Designed and created interactive Power BI reports and dashboards.\n\nSolution Benefits: - Established a consolidated reporting platform.
- Provided analytical reports for all operations.
- Enabled near real-time insights from reports.
- Automated reports across the organization.
- Developed interactive dashboards, reports, and charts.
- Allowed report access from multiple devices and platforms.\n\nKey Phrases: Power BI, Business Intelligence, Key Performance Indicators (KPI), ETL processes, Data Warehouse, Data Visualization, Interactive Dashboards, Real-time Insights, Automated Reports\n\nTechnology: Power BI\n\nClient Location: Long Beach, California
